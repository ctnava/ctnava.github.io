Design Philosophy

Holistic Design

No token designed without purpose

Tokens are a tool to receive funding; a resource used to build platforms. Platforms are tools to make things happen. Every contract comes with documentation.

Modular Contracts

No contract designed without fallbacks

Deployments cause stress on the network and raise gas prices. My contracts minimize their impact by using predeployed contract modules; meaning guaranteed savings.

Perpetually Reusable

Absolutely no planned obsolescence

Gas is a precious resource. Every contract is designed to be forever operable and profitable. By default, they are even made to be trustlessly exchanged.
